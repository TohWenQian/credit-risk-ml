About Our Mission

Leveraging machine learning to drive financial inclusion and empower small and medium enterprises through transparent, data-driven credit risk analysis.

Who We Are

CRA was built to transform how SME creditworthiness is assessed. We use machine learning — a type of technology that learns from historical financial data — to streamline and significantly reduce the cost of credit evaluation. Our goal is to enable faster, fairer, and more accessible financing for small and medium enterprises.

Our Vision

We are committed to leveraging the power of AI to drive financial inclusion. By making credit assessment more efficient and transparent, we help lenders make better decisions and businesses secure the capital they need to grow. We believe financial opportunity should be driven by data, not limited by manual, costly processes.

Our Technology

Our system uses XGBoost — an industry-leading gradient boosting algorithm — to produce accurate, multi-class credit risk predictions from key financial ratios. We pair this with LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations) to generate per-assessment feature-importance charts, so every rating comes with a clear, human-readable explanation of the factors that influenced it.

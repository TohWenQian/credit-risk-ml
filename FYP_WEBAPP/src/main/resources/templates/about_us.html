About Our Mission
CRA aims to use machine learning, a type of technology that learns from data, to streamline and reduce the costs of this process for SMEs. We aim to build a model that can swiftly evaluate the creditworthiness of SMEs, enabling easier access to financing for them.
At CRA, we are committed to leveraging the power of machine learning to drive financial inclusion and empower small and medium enterprises. Our goal is to make financial assessment more efficient and accessible, ultimately fostering economic growth and development.

How It Works

Submit the company's financial data — including cash position, EBIT ratios, net cash flow, debt levels, and employee count. Our XGBoost model analyses these inputs against patterns learned from thousands of real SME cases to assign a risk class. LIME then explains the top contributing factors in a visual chart, giving you full transparency into the decision.

What You Get

Each assessment produces a risk classification (e.g. Low, High, Very High, Default) and a LIME feature-importance chart showing which financial metrics drove the result. Assessments are saved per company, so you can track changes in creditworthiness over time and build an audit trail.
